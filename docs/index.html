Nov. 9th 1724

Order'd by the Curators of the Bodleian Library

  • 1. That the MSS. supra and Superius Seld now under
    the Inspection of the Senr. Proctor be Professor
    of Gr.     be transfer'd to the Senior Proctor; and that
    both of them have new Curating Books finish'd
    before Nov.r next.
  • 2. That all the MSS. which are out of repair in their
    binding be repair'd or new bound.
  • That the Rest of the Books in the Library below
    Stairs, be placed according to their sizes (as
    near as may be) in the same manner with those
    in Arts under the Inspection of the Senr. Proctor.

Order'd also that this Visitation
be adjourn'd 'till this day-
fortnight between Eight &
Nine in the morning.		 Rob: Shippen. Vice-Can
Po. Oxford R. Pr. of Divinity
Tho: Terry Gr. Ling. Pr. Reg.
James Bouchier Reg. Prof. of Civ. Law

to yͤ Librarian according to yͤ Direction of yͤ sd. Delegates by

Feb.14.174¾ At a general meeting of yͤ Delegates
of the Press, it was order'd, that every Author and Editor
who shall hereafter publish any Book from the University
Press shall deliver one copy of said Book Bound to the
chief Librarian of the Bodleian Library for the use
of the said Library before the Publication of the said
Book and that a Copy of this Order be given to
the said Chief Librarian.

Sign'd W. Hodges V.C.
Jo. Mather
St. Niblett
